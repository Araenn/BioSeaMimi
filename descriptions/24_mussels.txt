During a scuba diving expedition, the lead scientist of Mussel Polymers Inc. uncovered the impressive ability of mussels to adhere to surfaces even in strong currents. This revelation led to the exploration of the adhesive used by mussels, and in collaboration with other researchers and universities, a solution was devised that mimics this extraordinary compound. The resulting adhesive proves to be three times stronger than other underwater solutions, as noted by Mussel Polymers Inc., and is environmentally non-toxic.
